VAGAMON

On my first trip to Vagamon was wonderful experience,Vagamon makes for a great place to indulge in some hardcore adventure. There are 3 routes for off-roading: one that takes you through the grass meadows (Uluppunni), another one that takes you through the thick pine forests, and a third one, which is strictly for professional off-roaders, I returned to experience slow travel for the first time.
